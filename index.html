OUR MISSION

The EllyxTenzin Sophomore Caucus Team is dedicated to bringing you a fun, successful sophomore year by planning events that bring the student body together as well as creating resources that support the wellbeing and academic/extracurricular success of sophomores. Our team is complete with a professional, experienced board and committed members that are always working hard to bring you an amazing sophomore year!
